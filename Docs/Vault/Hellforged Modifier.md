# Hellforged Modifier

> [!info] Status
> Planned itemization feature; not yet implemented.

Hellforged Modifiers are special modifiers added to items by the [[Hellforge]]. They are additional modifiers and may provide effects unavailable through ordinary item modification.

The quality of possible modifiers is influenced by performance in the [[Hellforge Gauntlet]], especially the amount of Essence of Hell collected.

An item that receives one becomes [[Hellforged]] and cannot be modified further.
