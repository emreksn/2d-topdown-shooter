# Hellmaster

> [!info] Status
> Planned boss archetype; not yet implemented.

Hellmasters are among Hell's strongest soldiers. A Hellmaster is the boss archetype used for the final encounter of a [[Hellforge Gauntlet]].

Its power and rewards scale with the Essence of Hell collected during the gauntlet. Access to the [[Hellforge]] is granted only after every spawned Hellmaster is defeated.
