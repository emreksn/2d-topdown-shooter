# The Tower

> [!info] Status
> Planned content; not yet implemented.

The Tower is a sealed structure guarded by a powerful gatekeeper. The gatekeeper can maintain the seal but cannot clear the Tower.

When the player enters, the gatekeeper holds one of the player's items. The player chooses a modification after each cleared floor. The player may then leave and reclaim the improved item or continue upward and risk the run.

## Encounter configuration

The source that grants the encounter determines its number of floors, for example: `Adds a 6-floor Tower encounter to the next wave.`

Floors draw from a pool of monster themes. Themes are generally selected randomly, although some outcomes may force a predetermined monster pool.

## Risk and failure

The Tower is a push-your-luck encounter. Climbing farther grants more modifications and stronger final rewards, but exposes the player to increasing danger.

If the player dies, the run ends and the committed item is lost with it.

## Final floor

The final floor contains the Tower boss. This boss drops Tower-specific currencies that reproduce the gatekeeper's item-modification powers.
