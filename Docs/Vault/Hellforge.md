# Hellforge

> [!info] Status
> Planned item-crafting system; not yet implemented.

The Hellforge is a special forge assembled by Hell's strongest demons. Access is earned by defeating every spawned [[Hellmaster]] at the end of a [[Hellforge Gauntlet]].

It can Hellforge a compatible item, granting the item [[Hellforged]] status and attempting to add a [[Hellforged Modifier]].

## Rules

- The outcome is hidden by default.
- Hellforging is final: the item cannot be modified further.
- A no-change outcome is possible. The item gains Hellforged status without gaining a modifier and is effectively bricked.
- The amount of Essence accumulated in the gauntlet determines the maximum item rarity the forge accepts.
- Accumulated Essence may also allow stronger tiers of [[Hellforged Modifier|Hellforged Modifiers]] to roll.
- Item rarity and Hellforged Modifier tier are separate concepts.
- Only items with a dedicated compatibility tag can be Hellforged.

## Draft details

- The compatibility tag needs a final name. `Hellborn` is the current working name.
- A rare effect similar to Path of Exile's Hinekora's Lock may eventually allow the player to preview the outcome.
