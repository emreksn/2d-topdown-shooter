# Game Overview

> [!info] Status
> Early playable foundation.

The game is a 2D top-down, wave-based action shooter built in Godot. The player moves freely while equipped weapons automatically target and attack nearby enemies. Enemy packs spawn outside the visible play area, and the player survives timed waves before clearing remaining enemies.

The long-term design combines an accessible survival-action core with build-focused itemization and optional content encounters that offer distinct risk and reward.

## Current playable foundation

- Launch menu with Play, Options, and Quit actions.
- Starter weapon choice at the beginning of a run.
- WASD movement with a movement squash animation.
- Automatic nearest-target weapon behavior.
- Two-slot [[Weapon System|weapon loadout]] with shop-only weapon acquisition.
- Projectile and melee damage.
- Chasing and wandering enemy behaviors.
- Timed, budget-driven waves and enemy packs.
- Spawn warnings and a wave HUD.
- Extensible stats, modifiers, damage types, conversions, resistances, and monster scaling.
- Shop phase between waves with [[Items]], inventory access, and [[Relics]].
- Rarity-scaled shop weapons and generated weapon affixes.
- Optional [[Rift]] Content offers between waves.

## Planned progression pillars

- [[Active Skills]], with up to two equipped for use during a wave and loadout changes between rounds.
- Additional specialized [[Content]] encounters.
- Bosses and encounter-specific rewards.
- Endgame crafting and item modification, including the [[Hellforge]].
