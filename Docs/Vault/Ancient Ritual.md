# Ancient Ritual

> [!info] Status
> Planned content; not yet implemented.

An Ancient Ritual begins when a shaman appears and starts performing a forbidden ritual. Special ancient monsters spawn during the encounter. Killing them contributes sacrificial blood to the ritual.

Sacrificial blood is a hidden encounter score. The player cannot see, spend, or directly interact with it; it is used to calculate the ritual's rewards.

## Chants

The ritual replaces the normal wave timer with a sequence of timed mini-waves called chants. Each chant presents a choice that alters monsters, encounter rules, rewards, or final loot.

After clearing a chant, the player must choose the next chant within a generous time limit.

## Failure

The ritual becomes uncontrolled if the player:

- Fails to kill the required monsters before a chant expires.
- Fails to select the next chant before the choice timer expires.

An uncontrolled ritual opens a pathway to the undead realm and spawns powerful monsters. The player loses all end-of-ritual rewards, but these monsters drop ordinary loot.

Uncontrolled monsters are unaffected by Ancient Ritual modifiers. Normal monster modifiers still apply.

## Rewards

Ancient Rituals are primarily used to farm ritual-specific unique items and powerful ancient currencies capable of modifying items.
